About The Giveaway

Granger's Christmas Light Giveaway is an annual giveaway that Granger's Yard and Snow puts on to support the local community of London Ontario. We know the holidays can be a difficult time of the year for families struggling and want to help bring some joy to a family in need in the local community.

How Do I Recommend a Family in Need? It’s simple either post a video or write a submission anonymously ​explaining why the family is going through a difficult time and could benefit from our giveaway!

FAQs

When do I need to make a submission by?

We are asking for all submission by 12/1/2021.

What if I have multiple families that I'd like to recommend?

That's okay! Please tell your story for why each family would benefit from our help.

Why does Granger's Yard and Snow do this?

We believe that being a local company is more than just making money as a business. We have a opportunity and responsibility to help those in the community that are going through hard times.
